# Privacy Policy for Amico

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Amico ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access or use the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

When you create an account with Amico, we collect the following information:

- **Email Address**: Required for account creation, authentication, and communication purposes. We use your email to send verification emails and password reset links.
- **Display Name**: Your chosen name that appears in the App to other users.
- **User ID**: A unique identifier generated by Firebase Authentication to identify your account.

### 2. User-Generated Content

The App allows you to create and share content, including:

- **Events**: Information about events you create or join, including:
  - Event title and description
  - Event category
  - Date, time, and location
  - Maximum number of participants
  - Privacy settings (Friends Only or Friends of Friends)
- **Messages**: Chat messages you send within event conversations
- **Friend Connections**: Information about your friend relationships and friend requests

### 3. Device and Technical Information

To provide push notifications and ensure the App functions properly, we collect:

- **Push Notification Tokens**: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens and Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) device tokens used to send you notifications about events, messages, and friend requests
- **Device Information**: Basic device information necessary for App functionality (collected automatically by Firebase services)

### 4. Usage Information

We collect information about how you use the App, including:

- Events you create, join, or leave
- Messages you send and receive
- Friend connections you make
- Timestamps of your activities

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

1. **To Provide and Maintain the App**: To create and manage your account, provide core App features, and enable you to create events, connect with friends, and communicate with other users.

2. **To Send Notifications**: To send you push notifications about:
   - Events you're hosting or participating in
   - Messages in event conversations
   - Friend requests and friend-related activities

3. **To Improve the App**: To understand how users interact with the App and improve our services.

4. **To Communicate with You**: To send you account-related communications, such as email verification messages and password reset links.

5. **To Ensure Security**: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats, and to enforce our terms of service.

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Firebase Authentication, Firestore Database, and Firebase Cloud Messaging), which are provided by Google LLC. These services use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

- **Data Location**: Your data is stored on Google's secure servers. The specific location depends on your Firebase project configuration.
- **Data Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.
- **Access Controls**: We implement security rules in Firestore to ensure that only authorized users can access their own data and data they have permission to view.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

### Firebase Services (Google LLC)

We use Firebase services provided by Google LLC for:

- **Firebase Authentication**: User account management and authentication
- **Cloud Firestore**: Database storage for your account information, events, messages, and friend connections
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: Push notification delivery

Google's use of information collected through Firebase is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Firebase services may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP addresses
- Usage analytics (if enabled)

**Note**: We have disabled Firebase Analytics and Firebase Ads in our App configuration, so these services are not collecting data for advertising or analytics purposes.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

1. **With Other Users**: Your display name, profile information, and user-generated content (events, messages) are visible to other users according to your privacy settings and the App's functionality.

2. **With Service Providers**: We share information with Firebase/Google as necessary to provide the App's services. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

3. **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

1. **Access**: You can access your account information through the App's Profile and Settings sections.

2. **Correction**: You can update your email address and display name through the App's Settings.

3. **Deletion**: You can delete your account by contacting us at amico.support@icloud.com. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information, except where we are required to retain it by law.

4. **Push Notifications**: You can disable push notifications through your device settings at any time.

5. **Data Portability**: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at amico.support@icloud.com.

## Children's Privacy

Amico is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required to retain it by law or for legitimate business purposes.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
